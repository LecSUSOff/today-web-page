Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

I built Today because I value my own memories, and I believe your history should belong to you. This policy explains exactly what happens to your data. No hidden clauses, no data selling.

1. Data We Collect

To provide cloud sync and your TodayID profile, we collect the following:

  • Email Address: Used strictly for authentication and account recovery via your TodayID.
  • Journal Content: The memories and goals you write are stored so they can resurface at the scheduled intervals (7, 30, 365 days).
  • Profile Data: Your chosen username and profile picture.

2. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using an "Offline-First" approach. This means:

  • A copy of your journal stays locally on your device (Encrypted SharedPreferences).
  • If you use TodayID, your data is synced to Supabase (our backend provider). Supabase uses industry-standard encryption to protect your data in transit and at rest.

3. Third Parties

We do not share your data with advertisers or brokers. We only use Supabase to provide the database and authentication infrastructure required for the app to function.

4. Your Control

You own your data. At any time, you can:

  • Edit or delete any memory within the app.
  • Sign out to stop cloud synchronization.
  • Contact support to request full account and data deletion from our servers.

5. Children's Privacy

Today is a 0+ rated app. It is designed for everyone. We do not knowingly target or collect data from children under 13 differently than any other user. All data is handled with the same high standard of privacy and encryption regardless of age.

6. Changes to This Policy

As the app grows, I may update this policy. If I make significant changes, I will notify you via the app or email.
